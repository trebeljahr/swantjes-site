In Germany, 95% of the national peatland area has been drained for agricultural use. The resulting greenhouse gas emissions attract little attention. Voices of farmers and the economy are too loud.
But last semester I heard of the idea to rewater the peatlands and just cultivate different plants - is that a solution for environmental agriculture?

This is a short documentary about a few scientist, environmenatalists and dreamers of an agricultural reform.

Winner - Klimasichten 2020

Official Selection - LabMeCrazy Science Film Festival 2020

Honorary Mention - Rec Filmfestival Berlin - Youth 2020

Swantje Furtak finds a very creative way to illustrate her field of research vividly with filmic means. The video camera becomes a tool that documents the entirely subjective exploration of the subject area in a very refreshing way, and the montage brings the different aspects together in a narrative that is as exciting as it is entertaining.
